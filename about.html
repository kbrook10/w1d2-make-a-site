About Us

The Awesome Bowtie is about being the Bowtie and enjoying life.

There is a BOWTIE for everyone!

Back in 2000, not seeing bowties that I enjoyed I decided to learn how to make my own bowties. The journey started with many trips to Joann's and lots of finger pricks. However, after years of dedication and willingness to ask questions I now can design and make my own bowties. We just hope that more smiles are made by others learning how to wear and enjoy bowties.
